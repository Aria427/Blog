Some desserts are meant for everyday indulgence, while others are reserved for those moments worth celebrating. This Coffee Biscuit Gateau is definitely the latter.

Layers of coffee-dipped digestive biscuits alternate with a rich chocolate cream made from butter, cocoa, and a hint of brandy, all topped with a cloud of whipped cream. It's an impressive no-bake dessert that needs time to set overnight, making it perfect for planning ahead for birthdays, holidays, or any special gathering.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 200g plant-based butter
- 100g caster sugar
- 100g brown sugar
- 3 large eggs
- 100g cocoa powder
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 2 tsp brandy
- 3 tsp instant coffee powder
- 42 Schar digestive biscuits (around 3 packets)
- 250ml lactose-free whipping cream 
- Dark chocolate chips 

<b>Instructions</b>:

Chocolate mixture:

1. Beat butter and both types of sugar together at a high speed until a smooth texture is produced.
2. Beat in eggs one at a time at a medium speed.
3. While continuously beating at a medium-low speed, add cocoa powder at intervals until fully incorporated.
4. Slightly beat in the vanilla extract and the brandy.
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Gateau Chocolate Mixture](/static/images/sweet/coffee-biscuit-gateau_1.jpg)
</div>

Gateau:

5. Add boiling water to one teaspoon of coffee powder and mix well.
6. Slightly dip the biscuits in the coffee and layer the bottom of the baking dish with them.
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Gateau First Layer](/static/images/sweet/coffee-biscuit-gateau_2.jpg)
</div>

7. Cover with a layer of the chocolate mixture (half of it).
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Gateau Second Layer](/static/images/sweet/coffee-biscuit-gateau_3.jpg)
</div>

8. Add another layer of coffee-dipped biscuits (dipped in another teaspoon of coffee powder with boiling water).
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Gateau Third Layer](/static/images/sweet/coffee-biscuit-gateau_4.jpg)
</div>

9. Cover with the remaining chocolate mixture.
10. Add the last layer of coffee-dipped biscuits (dipped in another teaspoon of coffee powder with boiling water).
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Gateau Fourth and Fifth Layer](/static/images/sweet/coffee-biscuit-gateau_5.jpg)
</div>

11. Whisk the whipping cream according to package instructions. 
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Gateau Whipped Cream](/static/images/sweet/coffee-biscuit-gateau_6.jpg)
</div>

12. Cover the prepared layers with the finished whipped cream.  
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Gateau Sixth Layer](/static/images/sweet/coffee-biscuit-gateau_7.jpg)
</div>

13. Garnish with chocolate chips.
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Gateau Last Layer](/static/images/sweet/coffee-biscuit-gateau_8.jpg)
</div>

14. Leave the gateau overnight in the refrigerator to set.

<b>Servings</b>: 12
