Few dishes say comfort food quite like a homemade savory pie, and this Tuna & Spinach Pie is a deliciously nostalgic favorite in my kitchen. Inspired by classic Mediterranean combinations, it’s an easy recipe stuffed with hearty tuna, vibrant spinach, sweet peas, and salty anchovies, all enveloped in a gluten-free, lactose-free pastry.

This pie is perfect for a nourishing weeknight meal, prepping ahead for lunches, or sharing with friends at a casual dinner. It’s a satisfying bake you can feel good about - colourful, healthy, and full of flavour for every dietary need.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 1 portion gluten free savoury pie dough (recipe: https://nomad-gourmet-chronicles.vercel.app/recipes/4_Pie-Dough)
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 anchovy fillets, chopped
- 500g frozen spinach 
- 100g peas (fresh or frozen)
- 1 large can of tuna in brine (around 150g drained)
- 2-3 tbsp kunserva / tomato paste

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
2. Defrost the spinach and reserve the liquid.
3. In a large pan on low heat, padd the spinach liquid and onions. Cook until soft.
4. Add in the anchovies and cook until they dissolve.
5. Stir in the spinach, peas and tuna. 
6. Add in the kunserva and mix everything together until well combined.
7. Roll out half the pie dough on parchment paper and fit in to a pie dish.
8. Spoon the tuna and spinach mixture into the dish.
9. Roll out the remaining dough and place it over the filling to cover.
10. Place in the oven and bake for 20-30 minutes or until dough is golden and cooked through.

<b>Servings</b>: 4

<b>Macros</b> per serving:
- Protein      19g
- Carb         60g  
- Fat          14g 
- Kcal         442      

This tuna & spinach pie is wholesome, hearty, and packed with Mediterranean flavor. It’s a reliable comfort dish for any occasion and is friendly for nearly any table. Serve warm, paired with a crisp salad or simply on its own, and enjoy leftovers for a nourishing meal the next day.
