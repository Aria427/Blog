This tuna and spinach pie is comfort food at its finest. Inspired by classic Mediterranean combinations, it's stuffed with hearty tuna, vibrant spinach, sweet peas, and salty anchovies, all wrapped in gluten-free, lactose-free pastry.

Perfect for weeknight dinners, meal prep, or sharing with friends.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 1 portion gluten free savoury pie dough ([recipe](/recipes/4_Pie-Dough))
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 anchovy fillets, chopped
- 500g frozen spinach 
- 100g peas (fresh or frozen)
- 1 large can of tuna in brine (around 150g drained)
- 2-3 tbsp kunserva / tomato paste

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
2. Defrost the spinach and reserve the liquid.
3. In a large pan on low heat, padd the spinach liquid and onions. Cook until soft.
4. Add in the anchovies and cook until they dissolve.
5. Stir in the spinach, peas and tuna. 
6. Add in the kunserva and mix everything together until well combined.
7. Roll out half the pie dough on parchment paper and fit in to a pie dish.
8. Spoon the tuna and spinach mixture into the dish.
9. Roll out the remaining dough and place it over the filling to cover.
10. Place in the oven and bake for 20-30 minutes or until dough is golden and cooked through.

<b>Servings</b>: 4

<b>Macros</b> per serving:
- Protein      19g
- Carb         60g  
- Fat          14g 
- Kcal         442      

Wholesome, hearty, and packed with Mediterranean flavour. Serve warm with a crisp salad or on its own. Leftovers taste even better the next day.
