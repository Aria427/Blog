Blondies are a classic sweet treat, and this version has a nutritious twist - chickpeas add unexpected protein and fiber.

Easy to make, packed with wholesome ingredients, and happens to be lactose-free and gluten-free.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 1 can of chickpeas; drained and rinsed (around 240g)
- 125g peanut butter
- 50ml maple syrup
- pinch of salt
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 25-50ml plant milk (soy/oat/almond - whatever you prefer)
- 50g dark chocolate chips

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Pre-heat the oven to 180C. 
2. Line a small square cake tin with baking paper.
3. Add all the ingredients except the chocolate chips and plant milk to a blender and blend until smooth.
4. Blend in plant milk gradually until you achieve the desired cake batter consistency.
5. Stir in the chocolate chips. 
6. Pour mixture into prepared baking dish and smooth it evenly out.
7. Bake for around 20 minutes.
8. Cut into 8 squares and enjoy.

<b>Servings</b>: 8 squares

<b>Macros</b> per serving - based on 50ml unsweetened soy milk:
- Protein   6.5g   
- Carb      15.6g     
- Fat       11.7g   
- Kcal      198
