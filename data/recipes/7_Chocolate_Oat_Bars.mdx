Chocolate for breakfast? Absolutely - when it comes in these nourishing oat bars!

Loaded with rolled oats, dark cocoa, and sweetness from banana and honey, these bars are perfect for busy mornings or anytime snacking. Soft, chocolatey, and full of wholesome goodness.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 1 medium banana
- 1 large egg
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 400ml unsweetened plant milk (soy/oat/almond - whatever you prefer)
- 250g rolled oats
- 30g cocoa powder
- 50g dark chocolate chips

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Pre-heat the oven to 180C. 
2. Line a 8x8 baking dish with baking paper.
3. Mash the banana with a fork in a bowl.
4. Crack in the egg and add in the honey, vanilla and milk. Whisk to combine.
5. Fold in the oats and cocoa powder followed by the dark chocolate.
6. Pour mixture into prepared baking dish.
7. Place in the oven and bake for 20-30 minutes until set.
8. Cut into 8 bars and enjoy.

<b>Servings</b>: 8

<b>Macros</b> per serving - based on soy milk:
- Protein       7g    
- Carb          30.4g 
- Fat           6.2g     
- Kcal          206
