This creamy mushroom soup is comfort in a bowl.

Rich, earthy mushrooms blended with a silky roux and plant milk create a luxurious texture that rivals any dairy-based version. The miso paste adds depth and umami, making this simple soup taste absolutely decadent.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 700g mushrooms
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 600ml vegetable stock
- 20g miso paste
- 300ml unsweetened plant milk (soy/oat/almond - whatever you prefer)
- 56g vegan butter
- 28g gluten free plain flour 

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Roughly chop the mushrooms with the stalks.
2. Melt half the butter in a saucepan and add the chopped onion.
3. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the onion is golden.
4. Add the mushrooms and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
5. Add the stock and miso paste, and cook gently for around 15 minutes.
6. In a small saucepan, melt the other half of the butter and stir in the flour, cooking and stirring well for 3 minutes.
7. Mix the roux in the soup, add the milk and blend everything together using a hand blender. 
8. Serve and enjoy!

<b>Servings</b>: 4

<b>Macros</b> per serving - based on soy milk:
- Protein       11.3g    
- Carb          18.6g 
- Fat           11.2g
- Kcal          201

Serve hot for a light meal, or as an elegant starter. Even better the next day after the flavours have melded together.
