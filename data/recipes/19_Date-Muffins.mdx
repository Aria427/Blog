These date muffins are soft, fluffy, and naturally sweetened with medjool dates.

Soaking the dates in warm milk and blending them into a smooth purée means you get all the caramel-like sweetness without any refined sugar. With rice flour and ground almonds, they're naturally gluten-free too. Quick to make and perfect for a grab-and-go snack.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 13 large medjool dates (approximately 260g)
- 2 large eggs
- 250ml warm plant milk (soy/oat/almond - whatever you prefer)
- 100g rice flour
- 20g ground almonds
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp xanthan gum
- Optional: chocolate chips

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Preheat oven to 180°C and grease 2 six-well muffin trays.
2. Cut open the dates and remove the seeds.
3. Place dates in the warm milk and leave for 20 minutes to soften.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Whisk eggs, add to mixture and mix in.
6. Sift in rice flour, ground almonds, baking powder and xanthan gum. Mix until a smooth batter forms.
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Date Muffins Batter](/static/images/sweet/date-muffins_2.jpg)
</div>

7. Divide evenly into 12 muffin wells. Add some chocolate chips to each if you'd like. 
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Date Muffins Raw](/static/images/sweet/date-muffins_3.jpg)
</div>

8. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, until set through the centre.
<div className="my-1 w-80 overflow-hidden px-2 xl:my-1 xl:w-1/2 xl:px-2">
    ![Date Muffins Finished](/static/images/sweet/date-muffins_4.jpg)
</div>

9. Let cool completely before scooping out. Serve and enjoy!
