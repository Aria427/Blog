Zucchini in chocolate bread? Trust me on this one.

The shredded zucchini keeps this bread incredibly moist while adding nutrients, and you won't even taste it through all that rich chocolate. Made with oat flour and sweetened with honey, it's a wholesome way to satisfy your chocolate cravings.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 1 medium zucchini, shredded
- 1 large egg
- 90g olive oil
- 150g honey
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 130ml unsweetened plant milk (soy/oat/almond - whatever you prefer)
- 80g rolled oats, ground into flour
- 50g cocoa powder
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Pre-heat the oven to 180C. 
2. Line a 8x4 loaf pan with baking paper.
3. Squeeze your shredded zucchini of excess moisture with a paper towel then place shredded zucchini in a large bowl.  
4. Mix in the egg, olive oil, honey, vanilla extract, apple cider vinegar and milk until well combined.
5. Next add in the oat flour, cocoa powder, baking powder and salt. Mix until well combined.  
6. Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth top.  
7. Bake for 30-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out clean or with just a few crumbs attached. 
8. Cool pan on a wire rack for 10-15 minutes, then remove bread from pan, place on wire rack and allow it cool completely.
9. Cut into slices and enjoy.
