Sometimes the best recipes are the simplest ones. This tuna egg bread proves you don't need a long ingredient list to create something delicious and satisfying.

With just three humble ingredients - canned tuna, eggs, and a pinch of salt - you can whip up a protein-packed bread that's perfect for breakfast, snacks, or as a side. Naturally gluten-free and lactose-free, it's ready in under 20 minutes.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 1 large can or 2 small cans of tuna in brine, drained
- 2 large eggs
- 1/4 tsp sea salt

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Pre-heat the oven to 180C.
2. Grease two small ramikins.
3. Blend tuna with eggs and salt. 
3. Pour into prepared ramikins. 
4. Bake for around 15 mins (or until a toothpick comes out clean).
5. Take it out and enjoy.

<b>Servings</b>: 2
