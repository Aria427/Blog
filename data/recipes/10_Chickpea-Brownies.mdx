Brownies are a classic sweet treat, and this version has a nutritious twist - chickpeas add unexpected protein and fibre while keeping them wonderfully fudgy.

The cocoa powder and almond flour give these a rich, decadent flavour that rivals traditional brownies.

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 1 can of chickpeas; drained and rinsed (around 240g)
- 125g peanut butter
- 50ml maple syrup
- 100ml plant milk (soy/oat/almond - whatever you prefer)
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 25g almond flour
- 30g cocoa powder
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/4 tsp salt

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Pre-heat the oven to 180C. 
2. Line a small square cake tin with baking paper.
3. Add the chickpeas, peanut butter, milk, coconut oil and vanilla to a blender and blend until smooth.
4. Fold in the almond flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda and salt.
5. Pour mixture into prepared baking dish and smooth it evenly out.
6. Bake for around 15-20 minutes.
7. Cut into 8 squares and enjoy.

<b>Servings</b>: 8 squares
