Quiche is one of those timeless dishes that works for lazy brunches, weeknight dinners, or packed lunches. This version uses lactose-free cottage cheese for extra protein and creaminess, with a crisp gluten-free crust.

Delicious warm or at room temperature. Feel free to throw in seasonal vegetables or extra herbs.

---

<b>Ingredients</b>:
- 1/2 portion gluten free savoury pie dough ([recipe](/recipes/4_Pie-Dough))
- 4 large eggs
- 200g cottage cheese (lactose free)
- 60 ml unsweetened plant milk (soy/oat/almond - whatever you prefer)
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 2 spring onions, chopped
- 50g parmesan cheese

<b>Instructions</b>:
1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius.
2. Roll out the pie dough on parchment paper and fit in to a pie dish.
3. Add eggs, cottage cheese, milk, salt and pepper to a medium mixing bowl and whisk until smooth and combined.
4. Add chopped spring onions and grated cheese to the egg mixture and stir to combine.
5. Pour egg mixture into the prepared dish.

6. Place in the oven and bake for 20-30 minutes until set and golden brown around edges. It’s okay if the middle is still a little jiggly, the quiche will still set as it rests.
7. Let the quiche rest at room temperature for 10 minutes, then cut into slices and serve.
