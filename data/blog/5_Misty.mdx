Today, my sweet baby cat Misty passed away peacefully. She was energetic and full of life until the very last second, going strong at almost 18 years old.  She was more than a pet; she was my constant companion through childhood, teenage years, and now into adulthood.

From the day she chose me as her human—being born right on our doorstep when I was 12 years old — she brought endless joy and love into my world: chasing sunbeams, curling up on my lap any chance she got, and greeting me at the door like I was the best part of her day.

I can honestly say I don't know what to do without her. As I build a life full of new places and adventures, there will be a quiet space where Misty once curled up beside me, reminding me that some of the most meaningful journeys are the ones we share with our four‑pawed friends at home.

I can say that I'll think of her every single day, and she will never be forgotten — my forever baby, my home, and my heart.
